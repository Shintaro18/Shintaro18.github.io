パーツの印刷

Print parts of OperantHouse listed below under the following conditions. Small parts can be printed at once.


Nozzle size: 0.4mm
Layer height: 0.2mm
Infill density: 20%
Top Thickness: 0.8mm (if not otherwise stated)
Print speed:
   Initial layer: 30 mm/s
   Other layers: 100 or 150 mm/s (150 mm/s can make tiny gaps between traces on the top surface of the printed objects that can hold animal odors)


Use light gray PLA plus filament unless otherwise specified (PLA plus is a water-resistant version of PLA).
