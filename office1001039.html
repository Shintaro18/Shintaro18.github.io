Preparation(Download)

Prepare the following stuffs,

Tools (Necessary)

- FDM 3D printer (Print size: 220 x 220 x 250 mm or more)
 This tutorial is explained with Creality Ender 3 Max. This printer is a slightly older and may be difficult to obtain. Ender 3 Max Neo ($400 at Feb 2023) is a successor model of it so this may be the most compatible with this instruction (I haven't owned this printer).  

- Solder / Soldering iron

- Nippers (May be attached to Ender 3 Max Neo)


- Box cutter

- Instant glue

- Epoxy adhesive

- Masking tape (White)

- Double sided tape (Narrow)

- Hand drill and 5mm drill bit

- Phillips-head screwdriver (For M2 and M3 screw)
exp:
https://www.amazon.com/gp/product/B0925G9FFH/ref=ppx_yo_dt_b_search_asin_title?ie=UTF8&psc=1

- Wrench (For the 3D printer nozzle replacement)
exp:
https://www.amazon.com/Nozzle-Replacement-Printer-Change-Spanner/dp/B07F3S3RJH/ref=sr_1_3?crid=7NDOKIGRV3A0&keywords=3d+printer+wrench&qid=1677099020&s=hi&sprefix=3d+printer+wrench%2Ctools%2C89&sr=1-3


- Long metric ruler (60cm is best)


Tools (Useful but not essential)


- Plier


- Wire stripper


- Electric drill (should be compatible with 2, 3, 5mm drill and screwdriver bit)
